About US


SENTINEL SECURITY LIMITED was incorporated in 2015 initially providing dedicated, specialist professional security services to the banking sector and other financial institutions in Bungoma county and its environs. Sentinel has since grown to become a leading corporate security and protection solution provider, offering premium services to major private and public organizations countywide.

 

“Our objective is to be the preferred supplier of corporate security and protection solutions in Bungoma. This we do continually by developing and investing in our resources including our people, processes and technology. Sentinel is always consciously and deliberately adapting to the ever-changing needs of our clients.”

 

Our Vision

To be recognized as the county leader in providing security solutions

Mission Statement

‘To meet our clients’ security needs by providing integrated solutions. Our mission statement puts customers first, as we believe that the only way to succeed in the long term is by understanding and meeting the clients’ needs. We then focus on integrated security solutions having understood that we must provide a range of security products and services that will be pulled together seamlessly to provide the integrated solutions.

Value Statement

Sentinel is the leading county, integrated security company, specializing in the provision of security services and solutions to clients. Our mission is to create material, sustainable value for our customers and shareholders by being the supply partner of choice in all of our markets.
